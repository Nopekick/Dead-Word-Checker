Linking Verbs: DO NOT USE THESE

Linking verbs connect a subject with a noun or adjective that is the subject. An example of a linking verb is "are" in the sentence, "They are cold."

Examples: 'to be' verbs, become, seem, grow, look, prove, remain, smell, sound, taste, turn, stay, get, appear, feel
